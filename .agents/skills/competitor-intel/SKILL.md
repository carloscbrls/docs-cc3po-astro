# Competitor Intelligence

## When to Use

Trigger manually when:
- Updating pricing or packages
- Reviewing market positioning
- Preparing for a sales call
- Quarterly competitive review

## What This Skill Does

1. Scan competitor websites for:
   - Service offerings and packages
   - Pricing tiers
   - Technology stack indicators
   - Target market positioning
   - Content marketing strategy
   - Social proof (testimonials, case studies)

2. Compare against CC3PO:
   - Feature gaps
   - Price positioning
   - Unique advantages
   - Missed opportunities

3. Generate structured report:
   - Executive summary
   - Per-competitor analysis
   - Comparison matrix
   - Recommended actions

## Key Competitors to Track

- Dental IT: Pact-One, Darkhorse Tech, Lighthouse Practice Management
- Web Agencies: Local dental web designers, WordPress agencies
- Marketing: Patient Prism, DentalCMO
