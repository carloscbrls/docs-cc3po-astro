# Knowledge Base

Insights and learnings from running AI agent systems.

## What We've Learned

### Social Media

#### Best Posting Times (PT)
| Platform | Primary | Secondary | Avoid |
|----------|---------|-----------|-------|
| LinkedIn | 12 PM Tue-Thu | 4 PM Wed | Weekends |
| Facebook | 12 PM Mon-Thu | 7 PM Tue-Wed | Weekends |
| Instagram | 12 PM Tue-Wed | 6 PM Wed | Weekends |
| Twitter/X | 1 PM Tue-Thu | 5 PM Mon | — |

#### Content Types That Work
- Educational content: 11 AM - 1 PM
- Lifestyle content: 6-9 PM
- News/updates: 8-10 AM
- Carousels: 11:30 AM - 1:30 PM

### Content Creation

#### Blog Post Structure
1. Hook (first 100 words)
2. Problem statement
3. Solution overview
4. Detailed steps
5. Examples/case studies
6. Summary
7. Call-to-action

#### Optimal Length
- Blog posts: 1500-2500 words
- Email: 150-300 words
- Social posts: 100-200 characters
- Landing pages: 500-1000 words

### Website Audits

#### Common Issues
1. Missing alt text on images
2. No privacy policy
3. No terms of service
4. Broken links
5. Slow load times
6. Not mobile responsive

#### Quick Fixes
- Add alt text via batch process
- Use policy generators
- Run link checkers
- Optimize images
- Enable caching
- Use responsive themes

## Best Practices

3. Test in sandbox first

2. Give feedback regularly

### For Teams
1. Communicate clearly
2. Share successful prompts
3. Document processes
4. Review regularly
5. Improve continuously

## Common Questions

### How many agents do I need?
**Starter:** 5 agents for basic automation
**Pro:** 10 agents for full coverage
**Enterprise:** Unlimited for scale

### Can agents learn my business?
Yes. Agents are trained on your specific:
- Industry
- Target audience
- Brand voice
- Goals
- Preferences

### What if something goes wrong?
1. Agents monitor for errors
2. Operator alerts you
3. Issues are logged
4. Fixes are applied
5. Process documented
